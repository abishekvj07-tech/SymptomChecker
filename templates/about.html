About SymptomSense

SymptomSense is an innovative project designed to help users quickly and accurately identify potential health conditions based on their symptoms. Using state-of-the-art machine learning and a comprehensive symptom database, our platform aims to make healthcare guidance more accessible to everyone.

Key Features

Comprehensive Symptom Analysis

Users can input a range of symptoms to get an accurate prediction of potential conditions, supported by a large database of health data.

Personalized Health Insights

Our platform provides personalized recommendations, including medications, diet, precautions, and exercises tailored to each predicted condition.

Easy-to-Use Interface

Designed for user-friendliness, SymptomSense simplifies healthcare guidance with a responsive and accessible interface for users of all ages.

Reliable Health Data

Our symptom and treatment databases are curated from trusted medical sources, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for every user.

Technologies We Use

  • Machine Learning: SVC models for predicting diseases based on symptoms.
  • Flask Framework: A lightweight Python framework for building robust web applications.
  • Bootstrap: A responsive design to ensure a seamless experience across devices.
  • Pandas: For efficient data processing and analysis within the healthcare dataset.

Future Goals

We are constantly looking to improve SymptomSense. In the future, we aim to incorporate real-time updates on health trends, integrate telemedicine features, and expand our database to include even more health conditions and symptoms. We hope to make SymptomSense a go-to resource for reliable health information.
