Simply enter your symptoms in the input box and click "Analyze Symptoms" to receive tailored health insights.

Yes, we prioritize user privacy and ensure all data is securely stored and processed.

While SymptomSense provides insights, it is always recommended to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
